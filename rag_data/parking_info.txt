PARKING FACILITY - GENERAL INFORMATION
=======================================

Location:
We are located at 123 Main Street, Downtown District. Easy access from Highway 101. The entrance is on Oak Avenue, next to the City Mall.

Getting here:
From the north take Highway 101 exit 5; follow signs to Downtown. From the south use Oak Avenue. Public transport: Bus 12 and Tram 3 stop at Oak Avenue. The facility is a 5-minute walk from Central Station.

Parking area map:
Level P1 (ground) is for standard spaces; level B1 is premium covered. Clear signage at every aisle. Disabled bays are near the elevators on both levels.

Parking details:
We have 200 total parking spaces: 100 standard outdoor spaces and 100 premium covered spaces. Each space is clearly marked. Premium spaces are in the underground level (B1).

Space types and dimensions:
Standard spaces are 2.5 m x 5 m; premium spaces are 2.8 m x 5.5 m. Twenty premium spaces have EV charging points (Type 2). Maximum vehicle height: 2.1 m on P1, 2.0 m on B1.

Capacity and occupancy:
Real-time occupancy is shown at the entrance. During peak hours (weekdays 8–10 and 17–19) we recommend reserving in advance. Monthly holders have dedicated zones on B1.

Working hours:
The facility is open 24/7. Entry and exit barriers operate at all times. The customer desk is staffed Monday to Friday 8:00–18:00, Saturday 9:00–14:00.

Desk and support hours:
For in-person help, visit the desk during staffed hours. Outside these times, use the intercom at the barrier or the contact details below. Emergency breakdown assistance is available 24/7 via the intercom.

Holiday schedule:
The facility remains open on public holidays. Desk hours may be reduced; check the notice at the entrance or our website for holiday desk times.

Prices (see dynamic data for current rates):
Standard parking: hourly and daily rates apply. Premium covered: higher rate. Weekly and monthly passes available at the desk.

Rate details:
First hour has a minimum charge; thereafter billing is per 30 minutes. Daily cap applies for standard and premium. Weekly passes are valid 7 days from first use; monthly passes calendar-based.

Discounts and offers:
Early-bird rates for arrivals before 9:00. Monthly pass holders get a discount on additional daily parking. See the desk or website for current promotions.

Availability:
Current availability of spaces is managed in our system. Ask the chatbot to check free spaces for your preferred date before reserving.

Checking availability:
You can ask "show my reservations" to see your bookings. To reserve, provide your preferred date (YYYY-MM-DD) or a date range. The system checks free spaces per day before confirming.

Peak and off-peak:
Availability varies by day and time. Weekends and holidays often fill by midday. Reserving in advance guarantees a space subject to our terms.

Booking process:
To reserve a parking space you must be a registered user. Enter your nickname at the start of the chat. Then provide your preferred date or date range: single date as YYYY-MM-DD, or a range as YYYY-MM-DD - YYYY-MM-DD. The chatbot will check availability and save your reservation(s). You can ask "show my reservations" to see your active reservations.

Reservation rules:
One reservation per user per date. You can reserve up to 14 consecutive days in one request. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the date to avoid a no-show fee.

After booking:
You will receive a confirmation in the chat. No separate email is sent. Present your nickname at the desk if you need a printed ticket. Your licence plate is linked to your account.

Payment:
Payment is due on arrival. We accept credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payment apps. No cash.

Payment at barrier:
Insert your ticket or use the number plate reader; then pay at the machine (card or app). Monthly holders use automatic number plate recognition; no ticket needed.

Refunds and disputes:
Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days. For disputes, contact the desk with your reservation date and nickname. Unused pre-paid time is non-refundable.

Contact:
For support call +1-555-0100 or email parking@example.com (desk hours only).

Emergency and out-of-hours:
For access or barrier issues use the intercom at the entrance; it is staffed 24/7. For billing or account questions, email or call during desk hours.

Feedback and complaints:
We welcome feedback at feedback@example.com. For formal complaints, write to parking@example.com with "Complaint" in the subject. We aim to respond within 5 working days.

Accessibility:
The facility is fully accessible. All entrances and exits have ramps; elevators connect P1 and B1. Disabled bays are reserved and located close to the elevators on both levels.

Accessibility services:
Wheelchair-accessible payment machines are at the barrier. If you need assistance, use the intercom or ask at the desk. Accessible toilets are on B1 near the customer desk.

Electric vehicle charging:
We have 20 Type 2 EV charging points in the premium area on B1. Charging is included for premium daily and monthly users; standard users pay per session at the machine.

EV charging details:
Charging sessions can be started at the pillar with card or app. Maximum stay at a charging bay is 4 hours during the day. Full charging rates are displayed at each pillar.

Security and surveillance:
The facility is monitored by CCTV 24/7. Lighting is maintained on all levels. Report any incident to the desk or via the intercom. Vehicles are left at owner's risk; we recommend locking and not leaving valuables visible.

Bicycle and motorbike parking:
Bicycle racks are available free of charge near the Oak Avenue entrance. Motorbikes and scooters use standard spaces; discounted rates apply at the desk. Secure motorcycle bays are on P1 in zone A.

Lost and found:
Lost items are handed in at the customer desk. We keep items for 30 days. Contact the desk with a description and the date of your visit. Valuable items are logged and stored securely.

Loyalty and frequent parkers:
Regular users can register for our loyalty programme at the desk. Points are earned on every paid stay; redeem for free hours or discounts. Monthly holders get priority support and reserved spots in the premium zone.

Group and event parking:
For groups of 10 or more, or event-day parking, contact us at least 48 hours ahead. We can reserve a block of spaces and offer a group rate. Email parking@example.com with "Group booking" in the subject.

Maximum stay and overstay:
The maximum continuous stay is 7 days unless you have a monthly pass. Overstay beyond the paid time may incur an extra charge; check the barrier display when exiting. Repeated overstay can lead to access restrictions.

Seasonal and weather information:
In winter we grit the ramps and entrances. In heavy snow, follow signs for the alternative route. The facility remains open in all weather; contact the intercom if you need help in severe conditions.

Noise and conduct:
We ask users to keep noise down, especially at night. Revving engines and prolonged idling are not permitted. Violations may result in a warning or access suspension.

Temporary closures and maintenance:
Planned maintenance is announced on our website and at the entrance. During short closures we will direct you to the nearest alternative. Emergency closures are communicated via intercom and signs.
